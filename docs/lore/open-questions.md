# Open Questions

These questions track unresolved lore decisions. They do not block current canon, but they should be revisited before major story, faction, or campaign writing.

## Ethers

- What should the final name of the Council of Morality be?

## Magic

- Does Magic require Ethereal biology, training, artifacts, or realm access?
- Did Magic seep through bloodlines, land, language, memory, or some other foundation first?

## Maria and Airam

- Did Maria know Magic would let mortals kill Ethers?
- Is Airam still Maria in mind and memory, or did imprisonment transform her into something else?
- What does Airam want now: escape, revenge, protection of Nogias, punishment of the Ethers, or something stranger?

## Elves

- Did the Elves know Maria gave them forbidden power?
- Do Elves worship Maria, fear her, hide her name, or know nothing about her?
- Did Magic make Elves more willing to fight, or did it only let them preserve their seclusion?
- Which Elven teachers first trusted Humans with Magic?

## Lidium and Hell

- Do demons want freedom, revenge on the Ethers, conquest of Nogias, or some mixture?
- What exact rituals, structures, or corrupted lands can widen portals enough for archdemons?

## Nogias

- Did Beastmen, Elves, and Humans know they were created?
- Are there other original races besides the first three?
- How much time passes between Maria's fall and the later portal events?
- How did different Human cultures interpret rare Human magicians before Alfredo?
