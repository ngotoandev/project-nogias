# Glossary

## Ethers

Status: stable concept, working name.

The ancient race that conquered their known universe, sealed all Ethereal consciousnesses inside immortal avatar bodies, and eventually became the last species known to themselves. Humans later call them Gods.

Their knowledge is vast, but time has corroded their minds. They are powerful and arrogant, not omniscient. They are only all-powerful within their known universe. No one knows how much of their original kindness remains except the Ethers themselves.

## Council of Morality

Status: stable concept, needs naming review.

The working name for Ethereum's ruling class: the seven mightiest Ethers. All Ethers became immortal avatar bodies, but this council holds the highest authority.

## Nogias

Status: stable concept, working name.

The created mortal world where Beastmen, Elves, Humans, and other races live. Built by the Ethers as a hidden world far enough away to conceal their presence but close enough to observe.

The Ethers view Nogias as property and as a fun experiment rather than an equal civilization.

## Maria

Status: stable concept, needs naming review.

One of the great Ethers. Maria broke the law of non-intervention by secretly teaching Magic to the Elves when they were being destroyed by Human expansion. Her motive was compassion and a mother-like attachment to the Elves.

## Airam

Status: stable concept, needs naming review.

The condemned form or title of Maria after her conviction. Known as "The Original Sin." The name mirrors Maria and marks her fall from Ether to prisoner.

## Lidium

Status: stable concept, working name.

The parallel prison-world created by the Ethers to imprison Maria/Airam. It later receives other dissident Ethers. Lidium is the official name; Hell is the more common Human religious name.

## Magic

Status: stable concept.

The metaphysical force kept private by the Ethers. It was their weapon and last defense against created races. Maria teaching Magic to the Elves gave mortals the ability to threaten or kill their creators.

Once Magic entered Nogias, it seeped through the world and could not be cleanly removed. Magic can harm Ethers because it bypasses or affects what their physical-attack-negating avatar bodies cannot protect.

## Ether Avatar Body

Status: stable concept, needs naming review.

The immortal body that contains an Ether's sealed consciousness. It is made from a material that negates all physical attacks, which is why ordinary weapons cannot harm Ethers.

## Beastmen

Status: stable concept, working name.

One of the three original races of Nogias. Beastmen inherited the Ethers' physical prowess, became arrogant and aggressive, and dominated early history through strength.

## Elves

Status: stable concept, working name.

One of the three original races of Nogias. Elves inherited the Ethers' wisdom, became peaceful and secluded, and were favored by the Ethers. Maria taught them Magic after Human expansion nearly destroyed them.

Elves are not weak. They follow a doctrine against escalation because they believe violence changes the world permanently.

## Humans

Status: stable concept.

One of the three original races of Nogias. Humans inherited the Ethers' ambition and curiosity. They began as the weakest race but adapted, organized, expanded, and eventually dominated Nogias.

Humans later learned rare Magic from Elves. Some Humans sincerely seek wisdom, and the Elves who trusted those Humans taught them Magic out of appreciation for knowledge. Only very few Humans can actually conjure Magic.

## Ethereum

Status: needs naming review.

The official name of the hidden realm of the Ethers. Humans commonly call it Heaven. Alfredo accidentally opens a gate to Ethereum while practicing rare Human Magic.

Ethereum has a hierarchy. Its current ruling class is the Council of Morality, a working name for the seven mightiest Ethers.

## Alfredo

Status: needs naming review.

One of the most prevalent Human magicians ever produced. While practicing Magic, Alfredo accidentally finds a resonance that opens a gate to Ethereum and triggers the event later known as the Advent of Misery.

## Archdemons

Status: stable concept, working name.

Former dissident or rebellious Ethers cast into Lidium after Maria. Some worship Airam, some hate her, and some blame her. After millennia of suffering, archdemons adapted and learned to create many kinds of demons to build armies. Their soul-mass or authority is too great for ordinary portals, so they must widen portal wounds before fully entering Nogias.

## Demons

Status: stable concept, working name.

Creatures created by archdemons after millennia of suffering and adaptation in Lidium. Demons come in many kinds and serve as the armies that can pass into Nogias before archdemons can fully enter.

## Gods

Status: human religious name.

The Human name for the Ethers.

## Heaven

Status: human religious name.

The Human name for Ethereum.

## Hell

Status: human religious name.

The Human name for Lidium.

## Advent of Misery

Status: stable concept, working name.

The later name for Alfredo's accidental intrusion into Ethereum and the chain of divine retaliation that follows.
