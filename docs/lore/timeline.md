# Timeline

This timeline tracks working canon in chronological order. It is intentionally note-like and can be expanded as the lore develops.

## Ancient Ethereal Era

- The Ethers conquer their known universe a very long time ago.
- They seal all Ethereal consciousnesses inside immortal avatar bodies.
- Ether avatar bodies are made from a material that negates all physical attacks.
- A hierarchy remains in Ethereum despite universal immortality.
- The ruling class becomes the Council of Morality, a working name for the seven mightiest Ethers.
- They outlive all other species known to them.
- After a vast amount of time, they become the last species within their own knowledge.
- They accumulate an unfathomable amount of knowledge.
- Time corrodes even their smartest minds, leaving them powerful but arrogant rather than omniscient.
- Humans later know them as Gods.

## Creation of Nogias

- The Ethers grow bored with eternity, peace, and the absence of conflict.
- They decide to create a fun experiment that will produce action, movement, and change.
- They choose a planet far enough away to make direct travel difficult and hide their presence, but close enough to remain within reach.
- They create the world of Nogias.
- They create three races and grant each one a selected Ethereal trait:
  - Beastmen receive physical prowess.
  - Elves receive wisdom.
  - Humans receive ambition and curiosity.

## Early Racial Balance

- Beastmen use inhuman strength to dominate other races.
- Elves create weapons and traps that let them fight Beastmen evenly, but they prefer peace.
- Elves follow a doctrine against escalation because they believe violence changes the world permanently.
- Humans begin as the weakest race because they lack Beastmen strength and Elven wisdom.

## Human Rise

- Humans gather into towns to survive Beastmen attacks.
- Humans use Beastmen hide, claws, and fur to make armor and weapons.
- Humans send emissaries to Elven kingdoms to learn tools, traps, and craft.
- Humans build towns with layered defenses.
- Humans reproduce rapidly, form armies, and begin to dominate Nogias.
- Beastmen are killed off and scattered across the world.
- Elves are killed off and forced deep into forests.
- The Elven population is decimated, and some believe the Elves are extinct.

## Maria's Intervention

- The Ethers watch the collapse of the race they favored most.
- Maria, one of the great Ethers, breaks the rule against intervention.
- Maria secretly teaches Magic to the Elves out of compassion.
- Magic lets the Elves defend themselves and thrive again in the forests.
- Magic also gives creatures of Nogias the inherent power to kill their creators.
- Once Magic enters Nogias, it seeps through the world and cannot be cleanly removed.

## Condemnation and Lidium

- The Ethers discover Maria's intervention.
- Maria is condemned, tried, and convicted of treason.
- The Ethers create a prison specifically for Maria.
- The prison is called Lidium, a prison for sins.
- Lidium is a parallel world, like Nogias.
- Maria becomes Airam, "The Original Sin."
- Lidium becomes the prison-world for sins.
- Other dissident Ethers who oppose Maria's punishment or form forbidden attachments to Nogias are later thrown into Lidium.
- Some imprisoned Ethers become archdemons. Some worship Airam, some hate her, and some blame her.

## Human Magic and Portal Discovery

- Elves become highly proficient with Magic after Maria's teaching.
- Humans seek out Elves and eventually convince some Elves to teach them Magic.
- The Elves who teach Humans do so because some Humans sincerely seek wisdom, and the Elves appreciate the pursuit of knowledge.
- Magic becomes available among Humans, but remains very uncommon.
- Very few Humans can actually conjure Magic.
- Alfredo becomes one of the most prevalent Human magicians ever produced.
- While practicing Magic, Alfredo accidentally finds a resonance that opens a gate to Ethereum, the realm Humans call Heaven.
- The event later called the Advent of Misery.
- The Gods consider Alfredo's intrusion proof that the Nogias experiment is contaminated.
- The Gods seed incomplete dimensional-travel knowledge into Nogias.
- The new knowledge does not teach travel to Ethereum. It bends portals toward Lidium unless corrected by forbidden Magic.
- Humans open portals to Lidium/Hell.
- The realm war between Nogias and the prison-world.
- Lesser demons pass through the portal wounds first.
- Archdemons are too heavy in soul-mass or authority to pass through ordinary portals.
- Archdemons try to widen the wound through conquest, sacrifice, towers, rituals, corrupted land, or similar escalation.
- Archdemons create many kinds of demons to build armies after millennia of adapting to Lidium.
